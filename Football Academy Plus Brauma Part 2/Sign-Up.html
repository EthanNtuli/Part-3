Terms & Conditions

Please read and agree before completing registration

Participation & Safety

Players participate at their own risk. Football Academy Plus Bruma takes all reasonable precautions to ensure safety during sessions but accepts no liability for injuries sustained during training.

Code of Conduct

All players and guardians are expected to behave respectfully towards coaches, fellow players, and staff. Bullying, discrimination, or unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the academy.

Fees & Payments

Session fees are R200 per session, payable before or on the day of training. Gear and equipment purchased through the academy are non-refundable once issued.

Photography & Media

Plus Bruma FA may photograph or film sessions for promotional use on social media and marketing materials. Notify us in writing if you do not consent to your child's image being used.

Medical Conditions

Guardian must inform the academy of any medical conditions, allergies, or physical limitations relevant to the player's participation. The academy is not liable for incidents arising from undisclosed conditions.

Privacy

Personal information collected during registration is used solely for academy administration purposes and will not be shared with third parties without consent.

What happens after you register?

  • 📧
    Confirmation Email

    We'll email you within 2–3 business days to confirm your registration and provide session details.

  • 📋
    Bring Your Documents

    On your first visit, bring a certified birth certificate copy, guardian ID copy, and one passport photo.

  • ⚽
    First Session

    Sessions run Monday–Friday, 08:00–18:00 at 26 Hans Pirow Rd, Bruma, Johannesburg. R200 per session.
